# 🌑 PULSE_RUNNER

> *"Escape will make me God."*

**PULSE_RUNNER** is an LLM-driven extraction shooter set in the atmospheric dread of Tau Ceti IV. Heavily inspired by the cryptic lore of the classic *Marathon* trilogy and the high-stakes survival horror of its 2026 revival, this is a hardcore, tactical, and purely text-based experience. Built with TypeScript and a strict data-driven philosophy, the engine translates your tactical military prose into complex game state resolutions.

Forget the graphics. Here, tension is built through dynamic narrative, ruthless resource management, and the absolute weight of every decision you make in the dark.

## 📡 The Premise

You are a Runner. Your job is to infiltrate abandoned corporate complexes, derelict space stations, and quarantine zones. You go in, collect valuable data and technology (Loot), face corporate security and other Runners (NPCs), and try to find the extraction point before your oxygen (O2) runs out or your armor fails.

If you extract successfully, you get rich and upgrade your gear. If you die, you lose everything you brought into the raid.

## 🩸 The Heart of the Game

Unlike traditional text adventures (with A, B, or C options) or static RPGs, PULSE_RUNNER reacts organically to your tactical creativity:

* **Freedom of Action:** Describe what you want to do. Shoot the guard in the leg? Hack the door to lock him on the other side? Use an overturned table as heavy cover? The system understands your intent and resolves the action.
* **O2 is Life:** Time is not on your side. Every movement, every combat encounter, and every second spent thinking consumes your oxygen. Desperation alters your accuracy.
* **Brutal Consequences:** Combat is lethal. Armor breaks, bullets ricochet, and limbs are lost. There are no "bullet sponges".
* **Dynamic Narrator:** An AI acts as a ruthless Game Master, describing the environment, the impact of your shots, and the terror of your surroundings based strictly on the game's actual rules and consequences.

## 🧠 The Philosophy: "Human Thought, AI Programmed"

PULSE_RUNNER is a modern experiment in Game Design.

The soul of the game — the cruel combat design, the cryptic and oppressive atmosphere, the punishing economy, and the tactical tension — is purely human.

Artificial Intelligence operates on two fronts: as the **builder** of the engineering behind the scenes and as the real-time **interpreter** of your actions (Maestro) and the visual consequences (Narrator). The machine does the math, but the desperation of searching for extraction with 5% O2 is universal.
